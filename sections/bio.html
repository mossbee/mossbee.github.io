Biography

I'm a PhD student at MMLab, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, supervised by Prof. Chen Change Loy.

Previously, I worked as a Research Engineer at Samsung R&D Vietnam and as a Research Assistant at CMC Research Institute, under the supervision of Assoc. Prof. Nguyen Thi Ngoc Anh. She was also my undergraduate thesis advisor at Hanoi University of Science and Technology (HUST).

My research interests include Multimodal Learning and Reinforcement Learning for Reasoning and Decision-Making. I also have experience in Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, Contrastive/Metric Learning, and Language Modeling.
